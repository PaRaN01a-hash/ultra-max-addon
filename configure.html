How to get your free MDBList API key

Step 1 — Go to mdblist.com/register and create a free account.

Step 2 — Check your email and confirm your account.

Step 3 — Once logged in, go to mdblist.com/api

Step 4 — Your API key is shown on that page. Copy it.

Step 5 — Paste it into the box above and you're done!
